What's inside

Built for the way teams actually work.

Everything from first sketch to competition day. Profiles, projects, chat, calendar, resources, and recognition — in one place.

01
Member Profiles
Every builder gets a profile: skills, school, current projects, and role. Find the collaborator you need, or let them find you.
02
Project Hub
Create or join projects. Track tasks, upload CAD files, code, and media. See real progress, not just promises.
03
Team Chat & DMs
Project channels, direct messages, mentor threads. Smart notifications so nothing important gets buried.
04
Events & Calendar
Competitions, workshops, weekly challenges. RSVP, set reminders, see what the whole community is building toward.
05
Resource Library
Community-curated tutorials, guides, and templates. Searchable by topic and skill level. Anyone can contribute.
06
Recognition & Badges
Points, badges, and leaderboards. Active builders and contributors get seen. Good work doesn't go unnoticed.

Our story

Built for builders,
by builders.

Build Robotics Studio was founded by a small group of engineers who wanted one place to collaborate, learn, and compete together. We believe robotics education should be open, community-driven, and genuinely fun.

Mission & values
Mission
Democratize robotics education by providing a collaborative platform where anyone — from curious beginners to experienced engineers — can build, learn, and grow together.
Vision
A world where the next generation of roboticists has every resource, connection, and opportunity they need to build robots that change the world for the better.
Values
Open collaboration. Radical inclusion. Learning in public. Shipping real things. Celebrating failure as a necessary step on the way to success.
